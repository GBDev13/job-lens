Backend Engineer

About the role:
We're hiring a backend engineer to help build and maintain our payments
platform. You'll work on our API services and data pipelines.

Responsibilities:
- Build and maintain REST APIs using Python and PostgreSQL
- Collaborate with frontend and product teams on new features
- Participate in code reviews and on-call rotation

Requirements:
- 3+ years of backend development experience
- Strong knowledge of Python and relational databases
- Experience with cloud infrastructure (AWS or similar)
- Good communication skills and a team-first attitude

Nice to have:
- Experience with payment systems or fintech
- Familiarity with Kubernetes

What we offer:
- Salary range: $110,000 - $140,000 depending on experience
- Remote-friendly with quarterly team meetups
- Health insurance and 20 days of paid vacation
- Learning budget of $2,000 per year
