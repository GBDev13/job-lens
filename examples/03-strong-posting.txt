Senior Frontend Engineer (Remote, EU timezones)

Salary: EUR 75,000 - 95,000 per year, based on a transparent leveling framework
(we share the full band and where you'd land during the first interview).

About the role:
You'll join a team of six engineers building the customer-facing dashboard used
by 40,000 businesses. We ship continuously and protect focus time: no meetings
before 11:00, and we do not expect work outside your contracted hours.

What you'll do:
- Build accessible, well-tested React components with TypeScript
- Partner with design and product to shape features from idea to release
- Review pull requests and mentor two mid-level engineers
- Contribute to our design system and testing practices

What we're looking for:
- 5+ years building production frontends with React and TypeScript
- Care for accessibility (WCAG) and performance
- Comfortable giving and receiving thoughtful code review feedback
- You do not need to match every point below; we support growth on the job

Compensation and benefits:
- The salary band above, reviewed every 12 months
- 30 days of paid vacation plus public holidays
- EUR 1,500 annual learning budget and a conference of your choice
- Home-office stipend and fully paid equipment
- 6 months of fully paid parental leave for all parents

Hiring process:
1. 30-minute intro call
2. Technical conversation (no take-home longer than 2 hours, paid if longer)
3. Team and values chat
We aim to give a decision within one week of the final step.
